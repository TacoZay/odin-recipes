Pizza


This is prime time eats. Not only can the size of the pizza varies,
you can top it off with ingriedients that are easy to dice.
My favorite type of pizza is pepperoni pizza.

Ingredients:
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce *recommend making your own sauce
  • Flour
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil
  • Yeast
  • Water
  • Salt
Steps
  1. You have to make the dough. Start by mixing some water and flour.
    Mix in a bit of yeast and olive oil and mix until you get
    a soft, smooth, and dense texture.
  2. Flatten the dough out in a circle like shape or whatever you desire.
    As long as your able to place your toppings, it's ok.
  3. Spread some tomato sauce around the center of the dough.
    Keep spreading around the center until you are between
    1-2 inches away from the rim.
  4. Spread the cheese above the same areas where use placed
    the tomato sauce.
  5. Start to evenly spread the slices of the pepperoni around the pizza.
    Be sure to place the pepperoni on the cheese for it to infuse
    together with the cheese and sauce mixture.
  6. Place the pizza in an oven up to 400 degrees and cook for a couple mins.
    Check in intervals to see how done the pizza is being cooked.
