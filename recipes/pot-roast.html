Pot Roast

This compacted meal is loaded with a tender chuck roast mixed
hearty vegetables such as carrots, potatoes, and mushrooms.
This is the go to meal for a sunday dinner or whenever you
want to have some comfort food.

Ingredients:
  • Chuck roast
  • Beef broth
  • Red wine
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaf
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Vinegar
  • Onion
  • Olive oil
  • garlic
  • Salt
  • Parsley
Steps:
  1. For each of the vegetables, dice them up into bite size pieces.
  2. Using a large pot, sear the chuck roast. Pour a little bit of
    of olive into the large pot and turn the head up to
    medium. Then sear each side of the chuck for about
    2-3 mins each side until golden brown.
  3. Set aside the chuck roast and then place the onions in the pot.
    Cook until the onions turn golden brown and season with salt.
  4. Stir in some garlic for a couple of seconds.
  5. Using a 1/4 cup of red wine, pour in the red wine and deglaze the pot.
  6. Pour in the beef broth and a bit of vinegar into the pot.
  7. Place the chuck roast back into the pot and place the remaining diced
    vegetables into the pot. Put in some bay leaves, thyme, parlsey,
    salt, and pepper, then stir to combine.
  8. Place a lid over the mixture and then bring it to a boil. Place the pot on
    bottom rack of the oven and cook at 325 for 3-4 hours or until fork tender
