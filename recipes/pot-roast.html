Pot Roast

This compacted meal is loaded with a tender chuck roast mixed
hearty vegetables such as carrots, potatoes, and mushrooms.
This is the go to meal for a sunday dinner or whenever you
want to have some comfort food.

Ingredients:

  • Chuck roast
  • Beef broth
  • red wine
  • Thyme
  • Bay leaf
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Vinegar
  • Onion
